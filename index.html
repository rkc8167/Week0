Raise the Red Lantern

Interactive Performance

Raise the Red Lantern scrutinizes patriarchal dominance shown through a posthuman perspective. This interactive performance examines the Meishan body as an inscribed vessel shaped by traditional influences alongside surveillance mechanisms and silenced narratives. At her essence, Meishan embodies an exquisitely dressed woman whose intrinsic beauty stands trapped within an unseen, oppressive world. Ultimately, Raise the Red Lantern examines female empowerment and rebellion across physical and digital realms. The work prompts questions about whether our contemporary identities emerge from self-determination or simply another code written into the system.
